Poster Presentation Guidelines

Join the Eco-Revolution at IMMUNOCON-51

Say NO to Paper, Save Trees, and embrace DIGITAL POSTERS!

TODAY'S WASTAGE IS TOMORROW'S DAMAGE

Designing Poster presentation Designing effective posters requires careful thought and advance preparation. Identify the principal messages to be conveyed. Avoid enlarged abstract with few figures. Summarize current research in graphics forms: charts, tables, graphs, pictures, etc. Make it self-explanatory, you can then supplement and discuss important points

Below are few important guidelines:

  • Registration is mandatory for participation in Poster session and abstract publication in the E-abstract book.
  • Poster size does not matter in digital format. Even a smaller file when displayed on the large screen can be viewed clearly.
  • In the poster template we shared, the ratio of the poster is equal to the ratio of vertical screen.
  • A sample of the digital poster screen which will be provided at the venue. It is just a vertical placement of computer monitor connected to a laptop.
  • Poster structure: Title - Author Details - Background - Objective(s) - Brief Methodology - Results - Conclusion.
  • DEADLINE: Posters to be submitted 1 week before the conference i.e. on or before 27th September, 2023. Upload the posters via online form available on website.
  • Click Here to download draft to prepare the poster in this PPT format. Please do not remove any logo already put in the draft.
  • Each poster author will be assigned one of the e-poster screens. Each author will have net MAXIMUM useable space in a vertical format.
  • Time allotted for each porter presentation is 5 minutes.
  • In the default size of the presentation, please maintain the text size as mentioned below.

    - Heading - as mentioned in template

    - Author Details - as mentioned in template (underline Presenting Author's name)

    - Main text - Prefer font Arial or Calibri and maintain font size 18 or more (if space permits)

  • Use of Hi resolution images is strongly recommended.
  • Posters will be displayed full day, judges will review during the scheduled Poster Session. Time and slot details is communicated via emails and will be available on the conference website.
  • The presenter must wear an official conference badge while presenting the poster.
  • For each of the poster sessions, authors should be present at the scheduled time at their respective e-posters for discussion with the judges.
