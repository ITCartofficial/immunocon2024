Indian Institute of Science (IISc)

IISc Bengaluru, established in 1909, stands as a testament to India's scientific and research prowess. The institute was founded with the mission to foster innovation, excellence, and cutting-edge research in various scientific disciplines.

The institute has consistently been a pioneer in scientific research, offering world-class education and contributing significantly to advancements in science and technology. Its faculty, researchers, and students have been instrumental in shaping India's scientific landscape and achieving global recognition.

IISc boasts a rich legacy of groundbreaking research, encompassing diverse fields such as engineering, physics, biology, and many more. Its renowned faculty members are known for their contributions to cutting-edge research, exploring new horizons, and solving complex scientific challenges.

The institute's multidisciplinary approach and state-of-the-art infrastructure have facilitated collaborations and partnerships with leading global research institutions, fostering innovation and driving scientific progress.

IISc's commitment to excellence has earned it recognition as one of India's premier scientific institutions. Its consistent ranking among the top research and academic institutions in India and globally underscores its academic and research brilliance.

With a focus on nurturing scientific talent, conducting transformative research, and addressing society's challenges, IISc continues to be a beacon of scientific excellence and innovation, driving forward India's scientific aspirations on the global stage.
