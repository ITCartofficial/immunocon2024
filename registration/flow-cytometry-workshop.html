Flow Cytometry Workshop

On 16th October 2024

Jointly organised by Department of Biochemistry, IISc, Bengaluru and Trust for Education and Training in Cytometry (TETC)

Program Highlights

  • Basics of Multi-colour Flow Cytometry.
  • Applications of Flow Cytometry in Biomedical Research.
  • Considerations for Multi-colour Immunophenotyping.
  • Lab Module 1: Overview of Instrument, Instrument Setup and QC.
  • Lab Module 2: Instrument Setup, Compensation and Acquisition of Multi-colour Immunophenotyping Assay.
  • Tips for Data Analysis and Presentation.
  • FlowJo: Basic and Unsupervised Data Analysis.
  • Quiz and Feedback.
